About Our Company

Garcia Lawn Pro, founded in 2019, is a family owned and operated business serving San Antonio and surronding areas. We service both residential and commercial clients. Our owner, Gabe Garcia, started Garcia Lawn Pro so he can spend more time with his family and pursue his interest of landscaping. Gabe has over 23 years of lawn service experience.
